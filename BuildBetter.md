# BuildBetter

AI platform for B2B product teams that turns unstructured data (calls, tickets, Slack, surveys) into structured insights, reports, and context-aware PRDs with citations. Among the closest VC-backed players to [[The Full Discovery Loop|the full loop]].

## Company

- **Website:** buildbetter.ai
- **Funding:** `[Confirmed]` early seed of **$750K (Apr 2021; THCAP / Surface Ventures)** per Tracxn/Crunchbase. `[Unverified]` aggregator totals of ~$4.05M–$5.8M and a "$7M" headline conflict — treat only the $750K seed as confirmed. Crunchbase also shows a pre-seed (Oct 2021, Telegraph Hill Capital lead).
- **YC:** Not found in YC listings. `[Inference]`
- **Traction:** `[Unverified]` ~$770K revenue, ~7-person team (2025, GetLatka). Customer logos on site: Clay, Brex, PostHog, AppFolio, Zoom, OpenAI.

## Loop fit

`[Confirmed]` capabilities: ingests product/sales calls, support tickets, internal comms, surveys → weekly customer-issue reports, PRDs, and user-research docs **with citations**; turns standups into assigned action items synced to tools. `[Inference]` Strong on ingest → synthesize → draft-spec; less clearly autonomous on "recommend what to build + agent-executable spec."

> [!NOTE]
> One research pass initially flagged BuildBetter as possibly indie; Crunchbase confirms VC rounds, so it belongs here, not in [[Indie and Bootstrapped Tools]].
