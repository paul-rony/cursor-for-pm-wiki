# Dovetail

Customer-research repository and insights platform — stores and analyzes interviews, surveys, and qualitative data. Pioneered the "research repository" category; sits at the synthesis/storage stage of [[The Full Discovery Loop]], *before* recommend/spec.

## Company

- **Website:** dovetail.com — founded 2017 (Benjamin Humphrey, Bradley Ayers).
- **Funding:** `[Confirmed]` **$63M Series A (Jan 2022, led by Accel**, with Blackbird, Felicis) at a **>$700M valuation**; total **$71M**.
- **YC:** No.
- **Traction:** `[Confirmed]` directionally — 2,600+ customers (Atlassian, Canva, Shopify, Starbucks, Workday).

## Loop fit & a cautionary data point

`[Inference]` Strong synthesis/storage; an adjacent repository, not the full loop. Notably, Dovetail's **AI summary features draw the sharpest user criticism** in this space — see [[Where AI PM Tools Fall Short]] (Harvard URC hallucination test; multiple Capterra reviewers). It is simultaneously praised for synthesis-time savings (see [[User Testimonials]]), making it the best single case study of "useful copilot, unreliable autopilot."
