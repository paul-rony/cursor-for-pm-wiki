# Enterpret

A "Customer Intelligence Platform" that unifies all customer feedback (tickets, calls, surveys, reviews, social) into a quantifiable taxonomy joined with product usage and revenue data. One of the strongest VC-backed synthesis players for [[The Full Discovery Loop|the loop]].

## Company

- **Website:** enterpret.com
- **Funding:** `[Confirmed]` **$20.8M Series A (Dec 2024)** led by **Canaan Partners**, with Kleiner Perkins, Peak XV, Wing Ventures, Recall Capital + angels (Elena Verna, Nan Yu of Linear). Total **~$25M**.
- **YC:** No. `[Inference]`
- **Traction:** `[Confirmed]` directionally — customers include Monday.com, Canva, Notion, Loom/Atlassian, The Browser Company, Strava, Linear, Vimeo; doubled ARR since May 2024 (exact ARR undisclosed).

## Loop fit

`[Confirmed]` Strong on ingest → synthesize → quantify → surface emerging themes; **added AI agents to the platform (Oct 2025)**. `[Inference]` More analytics/intelligence than autonomous spec generation — it helps teams *decide* what to build but stops short of emitting agent-executable specs.
