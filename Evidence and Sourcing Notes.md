# Evidence and Sourcing Notes

Meta-note on how trustworthy this vault's claims are, and what could not be verified. Read alongside the confidence conventions in `CLAUDE.md`.

## Confidence tags (recap)

- **`[Confirmed]`** — named, datable primary/reputable source.
- **`[Unverified]`** — aggregator or single/conflicting source; not fact.
- **`[Inference]`** — author's reasoning.
- **`[Synthesis]`** — a paraphrase, not a verbatim quote (used in sentiment notes).
