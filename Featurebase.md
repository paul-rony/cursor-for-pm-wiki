# Featurebase

Bootstrapped support + feedback platform (feedback boards, roadmaps, changelogs, surveys) with AI features. An *input* layer to [[The Full Discovery Loop]], not a synthesis-to-spec engine.

## Company

- **Website:** featurebase.app — founders Robi Rohumaa and Bruno Hiis (Tallinn, Estonia).
- **Funding status:** `[Confirmed]` "fully bootstrapped and profitable"; has not raised funding. Founder blog: "Bootstrapping our startup to 100K ARR."
  - `[Unverified]` A search snippet mentioned an April 2025 "M&A Offer" — ambiguous (an offer received, not necessarily accepted); does not indicate VC funding.
- **Pricing:** `[Unverified]` (third-party comparison) ~$29/$59/$99 per seat/mo (Growth/Pro/Enterprise), billed annually.
- **Traction:** `[Confirmed]` public milestones — "0 → ~$4K MRR" early; "$54.5K revenue with a 3-person team in 2024" (GetLatka).

## Loop fit

`[Inference]` Voice-of-customer aggregation with "basic" AI (per third-party comparisons). Same category as [[UserJot]] and Canny — feeds discovery, doesn't generate PRDs or recommend roadmaps.
