# Granola

AI notepad for meetings — transcribes conversations, enhances the user's own notes, and makes meetings searchable and queryable (a "second brain"). Sits at the **ingest / meeting-intelligence** stage of [[The Full Discovery Loop]] — a *source* of customer and team signal, not the recommend-what-to-build / emit-specs engine.

## Company

- **Website:** granola.ai — **London, UK**; founded **March 2023** by **Chris Pedregal** and **Sam Stephenson**. `[Confirmed]`
- **Funding:** `[Confirmed]` **$43M Series B at a $250M valuation (announced 2025-05-14), led by NFDG (Nat Friedman & Daniel Gross)**; existing investors Lightspeed (Mike Mignano) and Spark Capital (Nabeel Hyatt) participated. **~$67M total raised.**
- **Notable angels:** `[Confirmed]` Guillermo Rauch (Vercel), Amjad Masad (Replit), Tobi Lütke (Shopify), Karri Saarinen (Linear), Des Traynor (Intercom), Lenny Rachitsky, among others.
- **Later valuation:** `[Unverified]` (single-source) — reported to have reached ~$1.5B within a year of the Series B. Do not present as fact.
- **YC:** No. `[Confirmed]` (absence)

## Granola 2.0

`[Confirmed]` Announced 2025-05-14 as "a second brain for your team." Adds **shared team folders**, **folder chat** (AI Q&A across a folder's meetings with **inline citations** back to source conversations), **multi-model support** (OpenAI / Anthropic / Google), **Slack** auto-posting of summaries and action items, and **templates** for use cases like user research and interview loops.

## Loop fit

`[Inference]` Granola is an **input layer**, in the same bucket the vault treats as feeding the loop rather than closing it — adjacent to meeting/conversation intelligence like **Avoma** and to the interview/research tier ([[Listen Labs]], [[Strella]], [[Dovetail]]). Its 2.0 **folder chat** pushes into the *synthesis* stage (querying across many meetings with citations), but it stops short of ranking what to build or emitting agent-executable specs. Strong, well-funded ingest/synthesis; not a full-loop contender. See [[Market Map and the Open Gap]].
