# Listen Labs

AI that runs thousands of voice/video customer interviews simultaneously — generating questions, recruiting participants, running interviews, and synthesizing results. Owns the **ingest** stage of [[The Full Discovery Loop]]; not a spec generator.

## Company

- **Website:** listenlabs.ai
- **Funding:** `[Confirmed]` Seed + Series A totaling **$27M** (both led by Sequoia's Bryan Schreier), then a **$69M Series B (Jan 2026)** led by **Ribbit Capital** (with Sequoia, Conviction, Pear VC, Evantic) at a **~$500M valuation**.
- **YC:** No. `[Inference]`
- **Traction:** `[Confirmed]` directionally — annualized revenue grew ~15x in ~9 months, crossing eight figures; customers include Microsoft, Canva, Alphabet, Robinhood, P&G, Nestlé, NBCUniversal, Anthropic.

## Loop fit

`[Inference]` High-momentum point solution for the front of the loop: AI-run interviews → synthesized findings. Pairs naturally with a downstream synthesize/spec layer rather than replacing it.
