# Market Map and the Open Gap

The synthesis note: how the "Cursor for PMs" market actually divides, why the full vision is still unbuilt, and who is closest. Builds on [[The Full Discovery Loop]].

## The headline finding

`[Inference]` **No company — VC-backed, bootstrapped, or open-source — verifiably delivers the entire loop** (ingest → synthesize → recommend → agent-executable spec). Everyone owns a slice. The connective synthesis-and-decision layer that the [[YC Request for Startups|RFS]] describes is the greenfield.

## The three layers

`[Inference]` The market fragments into three horizontal layers:

| Layer | What it does | Representative players |
|-------|--------------|------------------------|
| **1. Ingest** | gather/produce signal, incl. AI-run interviews | [[Listen Labs]], [[Strella]], Perspective AI |
| **2. Synthesize** | cluster, rank by evidence, surface themes | [[Enterpret]], [[BuildBetter]], [[Kraftful]], [[Dovetail]], Unwrap, Syncly |
| **3. Spec & hand off** | PRD → agent-executable tasks | [[ChatPRD]], [[GTPlanner]] |
| **(end) Roadmap** | prioritize/plan (incumbents + AI) | [[Productboard]], Aha! |

The "Cursor for PMs" prize is whoever **stitches layers 1→3 into one loop with evidence traceability and an MCP bridge to coding agents.**

## Who is closest

`[Inference]`
- **Synthesis-to-draft-spec:** [[BuildBetter]] and [[Enterpret]] are the closest *shipping* VC-backed players.
- **On-paper positioning:** [[Trova]] (VC side, funding unverified) and [[Sprntly]] (indie side, pre-launch) pitch the literal full loop — but neither is a proven product.
- **Spec authoring:** [[ChatPRD]] owns the output end and is the category's most prominent product — yet it is **bootstrapped, not VC-backed.**
- **Agent-native OSS:** [[Quackback]] is the only open-source feedback tool exposing feedback to agents via MCP.

## Funding & momentum signals

`[Confirmed]` Largest recent raises: **Listen Labs** ($69M Series B, ~$500M valuation, Jan 2026), **Maven AGI** ($50M Series B, Jun 2025), **Enterpret** ($20.8M Series A, Dec 2024), **Strella** ($14M Series A). `[Confirmed]`/`[Unverified]` consolidation is underway: Reforge↔Monterey (confirmed), Atlassian↔Cycle and Miro↔Reforge (single-source).

## Strategic reading

`[Inference]`
1. **The poster child took no VC** ([[ChatPRD]]) — evidence a lean wedge (spec authoring) is viable solo, while the *full loop* may need the capital the funded ingest/synthesis players are raising.
2. **Incumbents are vulnerable at the synthesis layer** — user complaints about [[Productboard]]/[[Dovetail]] AI (see [[Where AI PM Tools Fall Short]]) suggest bolted-on AI underdelivers, leaving room for AI-native entrants.
3. **The binding constraint may be human, not technical** — see [[The Judgment and Taste Critique]]. The winning product likely *augments* judgment (evidence + traceability) rather than automating the decision.
4. **The output format is contested** — recommendations vs. executable contracts (see [[Critiques of the Framing]]); whoever nails agent-parseable, evidence-cited specs has the defensible artifact.
