# Productboard

Product management platform whose "Pulse" AI analyzes customer feedback, identifies trends, and suggests priorities. A **roadmap incumbent layering AI on top** — adjacent to, not an implementation of, [[The Full Discovery Loop]].

## Company

- **Website:** productboard.com
- **Funding:** `[Confirmed]` **$125M Series D (Feb 2022)** co-led by **Dragoneer + Tiger Global** (with Bessemer, Sequoia, Kleiner Perkins, Index, Credo) at a **$1.725B valuation**; total **$262M**. No newer round found.
- **YC:** No.

## Loop fit & the AI-feature critique

`[Inference]` Sits at the *end* of discovery (prioritization/roadmap). Its bolted-on AI is among the more criticized: G2 reviewers report the AI features "do not yet provide real added value" and that "many suggested links of insight notes to features are incorrect."
