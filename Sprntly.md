# Sprntly

"AI agents for product managers" — five agents that synthesize signals (analytics, support tickets, sales calls, CRM, feedback) into ranked hypotheses, generate PRDs, prototype in Figma, and hand off to Claude Code/Cursor. The **strongest conceptual match** to [[The Full Discovery Loop]] of any tool catalogued here.

## Company

- **Website:** sprntly.ai
- **Status:** `[Confirmed]` **pre-launch** ("launching soon," opening to a small group of design partners). No founder name, pricing, or funding disclosed.
- **Funding/indie status:** `[Unverified]` — cannot classify as bootstrapped *or* VC-backed. Listed under indie provisionally; re-check at launch (it may be VC-backed).

> [!WARNING]
> Sprntly describes the entire loop — signals → ranked hypotheses → PRD → Figma prototype → coding-agent handoff — and even name-checks Cursor/Claude Code. But it ships nothing yet. Treat as a positioning signal, not a working product.
