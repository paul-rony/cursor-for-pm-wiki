# UserJot

Bootstrapped feedback, roadmap, and changelog tool for SaaS teams, with AI-powered duplicate detection and feedback consolidation. An *input* layer to [[The Full Discovery Loop]].

## Company

- **Website:** userjot.com — founder Shayan Taslim (also builds LogSnag).
- **Funding status:** `[Confirmed]` Marketing copy states "UserJot is bootstrapped and funded by customers, not VCs." Founder publicly identifies as a bootstrapped/indie maker.
- **Model:** free-forever plan plus paid SaaS tiers; positions as a cheaper Productboard/Canny/Nolt alternative.
- **Traction:** `[Unverified]` no hard UserJot-specific MRR/user figure confirmed; founder references growing "two SaaS products to decent MRR."

## Loop fit

`[Inference]` Partial — collects and *lightly* synthesizes feedback (AI surfaces similar requests as users type, consolidates votes, auto-tags). Not a PRD generator or autonomous recommender.
