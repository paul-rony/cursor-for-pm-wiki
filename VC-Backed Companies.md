# VC-Backed Companies

Tiered index of venture-backed companies building toward (or adjacent to) the [[The Full Discovery Loop|full discovery loop]]. Tiers reflect how close each comes to the complete ingest → synthesize → recommend → agent-executable-spec vision.

> [!NOTE]
> **No VC-backed company verifiably delivers the entire loop yet.** Companies cluster around pieces of it. See [[Market Map and the Open Gap]]. Funding figures are tagged `[Confirmed]` / `[Unverified]`; nothing is invented.

## Tier A — Closest to the full vision

* [[BuildBetter]] -- calls/tickets/Slack/surveys → cited reports, PRDs, user-research docs. Funding mostly `[Unverified]` beyond a $750K seed.
* [[Enterpret]] -- unifies all feedback into a quantified taxonomy joined to usage + revenue; added agents Oct 2025. **$20.8M Series A (Dec 2024)**, ~$25M total.
* [[Trova]] -- explicitly pitches "customer signals into specs your agents can build from." **Funding/YC status `[Unverified]`** — positioning match only.

## Tier B — Strong adjacent point solutions

* [[Kraftful]] -- "copilot for product builders"; feedback → user stories synced to Jira. **YC S19.** ~$5.15M total (varies).
* [[Listen Labs]] -- AI runs thousands of voice/video interviews simultaneously. **$69M Series B (Jan 2026), ~$500M valuation.**
* [[Strella]] -- AI moderator conducts + synthesizes interviews in hours. **$14M Series A (Bessemer)**, ~$18M total.
* [[Dovetail]] -- research repository category creator. **$63M Series A (Accel, Jan 2022), >$700M valuation.**
* **Unwrap.ai** -- NLP voice-of-customer synthesis. **$12M Series A (Jan 2025, Scale Venture Partners)**, ~$15M total. `[Confirmed]`
* **Maze** -- continuous discovery / user testing, increasingly AI-assisted. **$40M Series B (Felicis, Jun 2022)**, $60M total. `[Confirmed]`
* **Cycle (cycle.app)** -- product-discovery hub centralizing feedback. **$6M seed (Boldstart, Feb 2023).** `[Confirmed]` Reported acquired by Atlassian (2025) — `[Unverified]`, single-source.
* **Syncly** -- AI CX intelligence; fragmented feedback → trends. **$3.3M seed (SoftBank Ventures Asia).** **YC W23.** `[Confirmed]`
* **Perspective AI** -- conversational AI interviews at scale (founder Guy Nirpaz, ex-Totango). **$4M seed (Jan 2025, Village Global).** `[Confirmed]`
* **Reforge Insight Analytics (formerly Monterey AI)** -- copilot for product insights; Linear integration. Monterey was a **YC** company (~$125K) **acquired by Reforge (Jan 2025)**; Reforge reportedly acquired by Miro (Mar 2026, `[Unverified]` single-source). `[Confirmed]` for the Monterey→Reforge acquisition.

## Tier C — Incumbents adding AI

* [[Productboard]] -- roadmap/prioritization incumbent; "Pulse" AI. **$125M Series D (Feb 2022), $1.725B valuation.**
* **Maven AGI** -- enterprise CX/support agents (adjacent, not PM discovery). **$50M Series B (Jun 2025, Dell Technologies Capital)**, $78M total. `[Confirmed]`
* **Avoma** -- AI meeting/conversation intelligence; a feedback *source*. ~$12M total / $12M Series A. `[Unverified]` (aggregator-sourced)
* [[Granola]] -- AI meeting notepad; an ingest/meeting-intelligence *source*. 2.0's "folder chat" nudges into synthesis. **$43M Series B at $250M valuation (May 2025, NFDG)**, ~$67M total. `[Confirmed]`
* **Cursor / Anysphere** -- the namesake **coding** tool, *not* a PM product. Included only to disambiguate. **$2.3B Series D (Nov 2025, $29.3B valuation)**, ~$2B ARR. `[Confirmed]`

## Not VC-backed (cross-reference)

These are frequently named here but are bootstrapped — see [[Indie and Bootstrapped Tools]]: **[[ChatPRD]]** (the category poster child), **Canny** (~$3.5M ARR), **Aha!** ($100M+ ARR, never raised).

## Status notes / could-not-verify

`[Unverified]` **Zeda.io** (~$3.25M raised; reportedly shutting down 2026, single-source). **Notably** (~$1.5M seed Oct 2022, small). **Productlane** (no round details surfaced). **"Savvy"** could not be disambiguated as a PM company. **Marvin / Lookback / UserVoice** — no verifiable recent VC rounds found. **Replit / Lovable / Bolt** — coding/app-gen tools, no verified pivot to PM discovery; out of scope.
